How does the AI Resume Agent work?

add

Our AI uses natural language processing to extract your career experiences and rephrase them using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to create impactful, professional resume bullet points.

What is the STAR method and why is it important?

add

The STAR method is a structured way of responding to behavioral-based interview questions by discussing the specific Situation, Task, Action, and Result. It's important for resumes because it provides clear, quantifiable evidence of your accomplishments, making your experience more compelling to recruiters.
